About Us


Greenbuz is an event planning company and we specialize in corporate retreat training services as well as product launch and entertainment.  The corporate retreat training services can be either leadership development training or teaming skills training.  

For both types of retreats, Greenbuz can take care of the planning of the event, as well as actually hosting the training through the use of one of Greenbuz's strategic business partners.
Our event management services gives you exactly what you want - tailor made occasions that will bring your guests closer to you and help create relationships that last.
Everyone loves entertainment, be it musical, drama, comedies, art festivals, etc. This is an area where we tend to capitalize as in this regard out target is everyone. We reach out to everyone through our different events.

With Greenbuz, you'll have an exclusive eccess to a highly professional event planning team who measure their performance against your satisfaction. Our team will advice and assist you to realize your own special visions or help you to create one.
